Le ciel a Visité la Terre and Christmas Round
Catalogue Number:P195/003/008
Composer:Le ciel: Charles Gounod; Christmas Round: Leslie Woodgate
Title:Le ciel a Visité la Terre and Christmas Round
First Line of Text:Le ciel a visité a terre, mon bien aimé, repose en moi.
Lyricist:Le ciel: Charles Gounod
Extent:Le ciel: 1 manuscript (2 pages)
Combined: 5 choral copies (2 pages)
Language:French
Genre:Choral work, originally for voice and piano. Round.
Ensemble:SSA with soloist
Key Signature:F major, then E-flat major, then B-flat major
Time Signature:4/4
Tempo:Unknown
Range:Le Ciel: a to g2; Christmas Round: d1 to d2
Number of Bars:Le ciel: 17
Combined: 19
Physical Condition:Good, a little scattered.
Copyright Status:Public Domain
Other Formats:Scanned manuscript also available in digital format.
Notes:Instructions to sing it:
Introduction on Ah!
Choir sings first verse alone “Adeste Fideles”, take first end and return to (1)
Choir hums while soloist sings English works.
Everybody takes 2nd ending.
Choir goes up a semitone and sings Latin Words.
Soloist sings obbligato.
Take first ending and return to (4) with no breath.
Can be with or without organ.
-Perhaps, sung together with Noël de petits oiseux
