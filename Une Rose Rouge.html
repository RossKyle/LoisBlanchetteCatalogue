Une Rose Rouge
Catalogue Number:P195/018/003
Composer:Norbert Glanzberg
Title:Une Rose Rouge
First Line of Text:Une rose rouge rouge rouge rouge de printemps.
Lyricist:Francis Lemarque
Date:January 1962
Publisher:Version 1: Originally in France at MCMLIX by Editions Salabert S.A. in 1959
Extent:Manuscript: Version 1: 1 (5 pages) [has a new bridge written on the back of page 1], 1 page 2 of long version with new words after the bridge; Version 2: 1 (1 page)
Choral Copies: 1 copy with corrections (1981, then changed in 2004), Version 2: 5 (1 page)
Other: 2 pages of lyrics, and the copy the piece was arranged from.
Language:French
Genre:Choral work
Ensemble:Both: SSAA
Key Signature:Version 1: C major; Version 2: B-flat major
Time Signature:3/4
Tempo:Unknown
Range:Version 1: a to a2; Version 2: c1 to g2
Number of Bars:Version 1: 136, Version 2: 30
Physical Condition:Good.
Copyright Status:Copyright protected
Other Formats:Scores are available in the Bishop’s University Library.
Notes:-There are many corrections surrounding this piece. -There are two versions: one is longer, the other shorter. -On the choral copy of version 1 there is two versions of the start, one if 1981 and the other from 2004. The second version is fairly straightforward.
