J’ai Joué de la Flûte
Catalogue Number:P195/010/001
Composer:R. P. A. Duval
Title:J’ai Joué de la Flûte
Refrain:J’ai joué de la flute sur la place du marché et personne avec moi n’a coulu danser!
Lyricist:Bible: Matthew: 11-17.
Extent:Manuscript: Version 1: 1 (4 pages); Version 2: 1 (2 pages)
Choral Copies: 1 copy of Mrs. Blanchette’s Version 2 (4 pages)
Other: 22 copies of a version by S. Moisse (2 pages)
Language:French
Ensemble:Version 1: SSAA; Version 2: Sop divisi, sop mezzo, alto and contralto; Moisse: SSAA
Key Signature:D major
Time Signature:4/4
Tempo:Unknown
Range:Version 1: a to g2; Version 2: a to g2; Moisse: a to a2
Number of Bars:Version 1: 61, Version 2: 31, Moisse: 31
Physical Condition:Good.
Copyright Status:Public Domain
Other Formats:Scanned manuscript also available in digital format.
Notes:Version 1: shortened, the verses are written at the end, rather than written out like in Version 1. Written for Chorale de l’Amitié.
Version 2: Lots of corrections, and out of order (example: intro in written at the end). Rewritten for Les Souliers Dorés
Moisse arrangement: very similar to Version 2, but there are note differences.
