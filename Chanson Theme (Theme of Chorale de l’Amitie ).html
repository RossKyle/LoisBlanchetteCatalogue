Catalogue Number:P195/003/006
Composer:Traditional
Title:Chanson Thème (Theme of Chorale de l’Amitié )
First Line of Text:Hello chorale de l’Amitié chantons
la mésure étudiée, de toutes harmonisations.
Lyricist:Annette Rousseau
Extent:Version 1: 1 manuscript and 26 choral copies
Version 2: 17 choral copies
Language:English and French
Genre:Folk song
Ensemble:SSAA
Key Signature:Begins in D major, ends in E-flat major
Time Signature:4/4
Tempo:Unknown
Range:a to g2
Duration:Marked 1:20 minutes
Number of Bars:38
Physical Condition:A little torn.
Copyright Status:Public Domain
Other Formats:Scanned manuscripts also available in digital format.
Notes:Melody common at sporting events.
