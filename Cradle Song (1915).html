Cradle Song (1915)
Catalogue Number:P195/003/010
Composer:Fritz Kreisler
Title:Cradle Song (1915)
First Line of Text:You and I are all alone. I have only you. (Kreisler Lullaby: Thru’ the cold and stormy night angels watch on high.
Lyricist:Alice Mattullath (unknown death date)
Extent:Choral Manuscript: 1 (2 pages)
Piano Music: 2 copies for Kreisler Lullaby (4 pages), 1 incomplete copy (pages 2 and 3)
Other: Kreisler Lullaby arrangement: 1 (1 page)
Language:English
Genre:Voice and Piano
Ensemble:Cradle song: SSAA; Kreisler Lullaby: SA and piano
Key Signature:Cradle Song: G Major; Kreisler Lullaby: D Major
Time Signature:Cradle Song: 6/8; Kreisler Lullaby: 6/8
Tempo:Cradle Song: Moderato
Range:Cradle Song: g to f-sharp2; Kreisler Lullaby: g to d2
Duration:Unknown
Number of Bars:Cradle Song: 32; Kreisler Lullaby: choral 20, piano 29
Physical Condition:Good. A little confusing.
Copyright Status:Copyright unknown.
Other Formats:Scores available in the Bishop’s University Library.
Notes:Instructions: The Kreisler Lullaby piece is repeated three times: chorus only form A to H, then piano only from A to H, and then both play form B to H, then 3rd end at the top of page 4.
- The Cradle Song is a war song from 1915, Kreisler added new lyrics to Cradle song, and someone rewrote the lyrics
-This arrangement might have been done with the violin in the Chorale di L’Amitié catalogue, but she might have rearranged it with piano and violin for Souliers Dorés
